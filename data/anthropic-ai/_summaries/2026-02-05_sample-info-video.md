## Overview

This video provides an introduction to Anthropic's Constitutional AI approach,
explaining how AI systems can be trained to be helpful, harmless, and honest.

## Key Points

- Constitutional AI uses a set of principles to guide model behavior
- The approach reduces reliance on human feedback for safety
- Models trained this way show improved alignment properties

## Takeaways

Constitutional AI represents a promising direction for scalable AI safety,
allowing models to self-critique and revise outputs based on defined principles.
