# Narrative: Jordi Visser

Jordi Visser presents a macro-focused investment perspective with emphasis on understanding market cycles and structural trends.

## Core Thesis

- Markets are driven by liquidity cycles
- Technology disruption creates asymmetric opportunities
- Risk management is paramount in volatile environments

## Investment Framework

1. Identify macro regime
2. Assess liquidity conditions
3. Find asymmetric risk/reward setups
4. Size positions according to conviction

## Key Themes

- Global macro analysis
- Equity market structure
- Crypto as a macro asset
- Behavioral finance insights
