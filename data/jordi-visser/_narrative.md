## Investment Narrative: Q1 2026

Jordi Visser's current macro framework centers on the tension between
persistent inflation and slowing growth across developed markets.

### Core Thesis

The post-pandemic normalization is taking longer than expected, with central
banks navigating a difficult path between controlling inflation and avoiding
recession. This creates a challenging environment for traditional 60/40
portfolios.

### Key Positions

**Overweight:**
- Commodities and real assets as inflation hedges
- Emerging market equities with dollar exposure
- Short-duration fixed income

**Underweight:**
- Long-duration US Treasuries
- High-multiple growth stocks
- Consumer discretionary

### Risk Factors

The main risks to this view are a faster-than-expected inflation decline
enabling Fed cuts, or a hard landing scenario that would require significant
portfolio repositioning.
