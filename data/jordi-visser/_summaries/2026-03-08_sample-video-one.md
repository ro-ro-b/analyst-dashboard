## Overview

Deep dive into the relationship between interest rates and equity valuations,
with specific focus on current market conditions and forward expectations.

## Key Points

- Duration risk in equities often underappreciated by investors
- Current P/E multiples pricing in significant rate cuts
- Earnings growth must accelerate if rates stay higher for longer
- Sector rotation opportunities in value vs growth

## Analysis

Using a discounted cash flow framework, Jordi demonstrates how sensitive
current equity valuations are to changes in the discount rate. A 50bps
upward revision to the terminal rate assumption reduces fair value estimates
by 10-15% for high-multiple stocks.

## Takeaways

Investors should stress-test their portfolios against a higher-for-longer
rate scenario. The risk/reward for long-duration assets remains unfavorable
given current valuations and macro uncertainty.
