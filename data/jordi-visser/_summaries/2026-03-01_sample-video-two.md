## Overview

Jordi reviews the global macro landscape heading into spring 2026, covering
key themes across equities, fixed income, and currencies.

## Key Points

- US exceptionalism narrative showing cracks
- European equities outperforming on relative basis
- Currency markets pricing in divergent central bank paths
- Commodity supercycle thesis gaining traction

## Market Implications

The current environment favors active management and geographic diversification.
Passive strategies concentrated in US large-cap may underperform over the
medium term as global rebalancing continues.

## Takeaways

Maintain diversified exposure across geographies and asset classes. The
macro environment rewards flexibility and disciplined risk management.
